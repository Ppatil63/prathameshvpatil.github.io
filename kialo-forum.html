Who's Participating

Participant Groups

Displaced Workers & Recent Graduates

5–6 participants

Recruited via Reddit — r/layoffs, r/cscareerquestions

Individuals who have experienced or fear automation-driven job displacement, and recent graduates navigating a contracting entry-level market.

🔵

HR & Workforce Development Professionals

2–3 participants

1–2 participants

Researchers and policy analysts who contextualize lived experience within broader labor market and regulatory frameworks.

Community Standards

Ground Rules

🤝

RULE 01

Be respectful — critique ideas, not people. Disagreement is the fuel of good deliberation.

🕶️

RULE 02

Anonymous participation is allowed and respected. You may choose not to reveal your identity at any point.

📊

RULE 03

Use evidence when possible — links, data, and personal experience all count equally as contributions.

⚖️

RULE 04

Every voice matters equally, regardless of educational background, job title, or credentials.

💬

RULE 05

Respond to at least 2 other participants' points per discussion question to keep the dialogue alive.
